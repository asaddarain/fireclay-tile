Our Products

You won’t find our tile in big showrooms or third-party sellers because we believe tile should come straight from the people who know it best, our Makers.

Tile

Since 1986, we’ve been making all of our ceramic tile by hand, start to finish, nestled right in the middle of California’s agricultural heartland.

Glazed Thin Brick

A timeless material takes on a colorful new look with 28 standard colors to choose from. What’s more to love? 1% of all Brick sales are donated to US National Parks Conservation.

Quick Ship Tile and Brick

Need tile fast? Get up to speed on your next project with our best-selling recycled Tile and Brick, shipped from our factory in 5 days or less.

Handpainted Tile

Made with time-honored techniques going back hundreds of years, every tile is handpainted to order by our skilled Makers in Aromas, California.

Glass Tile

Raise a Glass! Our handmade Glass Tile offers a wide range of design possibilities for both residential and commercial applications.

Non-Slip Tile

There is no competition for our Non-Slip Tile. With a breaking strength of 393 pounds per foot, these tiles will withstand the rigor of commercial flooring applications while passing the DCOF Test.
